About

In 1990, National University in Bangladesh established the first-ever B.Sc. Honors program in Computer Science, marking a significant milestone as the inaugural department within the National University of Bangladesh.When the university was officially renamed, becoming the B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE).
