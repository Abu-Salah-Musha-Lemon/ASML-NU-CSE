Firstly, no professional course is taught in any government college. (Memorize it first.)
    Name and number of seats of all the colleges and institutes under National University Professional and the district in which they are located are mentioned along with the district name.
    Courses are available at all the colleges and institutes listed, but not all places admit students equally.
    Before taking admission you need to know how many people took admission there last year and whether the class is suitable or not.
    If less than 30 people it is better not to admit there. Because 7/8 out of 30 will drop out. 15/16 people will come to the class. You will not like to take classes with so few students and your exam results will not be that good. The class will not be suitable for less students.
    So, research the college well before admission and then get admission.
Note: College and its number of seats may be less or more.
